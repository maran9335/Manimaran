The town is located in south east Tamil Nadu Connected by NH 49 to Madurai from Rameswaram.and East Coast Road is the major coastal road in east Tamil Naduwhich connects the state capital Chennai and Ramanathapuram; this road also connects Ramanathapuram with Pondicherry, Tuticorin and Kanyakumari. The town is well connected by railroad to major cities in India through Madurai Junction.
