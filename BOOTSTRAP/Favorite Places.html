Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, celebrated for its breathtaking beauty and symbolizing eternal love.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, located in Amritsar, India, is a revered Sikh shrine known for its stunning architecture, peaceful surroundings, and the sacred pool that surrounds it.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, a magnificent royal residence in Karnataka, India, showcases Indo-Saracenic architecture, intricate artwork, and vibrant festivities during the annual Dasara celebrations.
